Answers

Frequently asked

If your question isn't here, text 570-677-7971.

What areas do you serve?
Northeast Pennsylvania: Clarks Summit, Scranton, Dunmore, Dickson City, Olyphant, Throop, Moosic, Old Forge, South Abington Township, Waverly, and surrounding communities. If your zip starts with 184, 185, or 186 there's a good chance we cover you. Text us your address if you're not sure.
How does billing work?
You pick a package, complete checkout through Stripe, and you're charged per visit on your chosen schedule. Weekly subscriptions charge once per week, bi-weekly every two weeks, monthly once per month, and one-time charges once. You'll always know what's coming.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No long-term contract. Cancel from the customer portal Stripe sends you after checkout, or text us at 570-677-7971 and we'll stop billing immediately. No penalty, no questions.
What if it rains on my service day?
We move you to the next available dry day, usually within 24-48 hours. If we miss an entire week due to weather, we don't charge you for that week.
Do you bag the clippings?
By default we mulch and side-discharge, which feeds your lawn for free. Bagging is available on request for an additional $10 per visit on Small/Medium yards and $15 on Large/XL.
Are you insured?
Yes. NEPA-PRO carries full general liability insurance. Certificate of insurance available on request for HOAs, property managers, and commercial customers.
Why an all-electric fleet?
Quieter, zero emissions at point of use, and we can start earlier and finish later without disturbing neighbors. Better for your lawn, your air, and your weekend.
What's included in every visit?
Mowing at the optimal height for your grass type, edging along all hard surfaces (sidewalks, driveways, beds), line trimming around obstacles (trees, fences, posts), and blow-off of clippings from all hard surfaces. We leave it cleaner than we found it.
Will the same person service my lawn every time?
Yes — your route is assigned to a small consistent crew so they know your property: gate codes, where the dog hides, the spot by the deck that needs extra care.
Do I need to be home?
No. As long as gates are unlocked (or we have the code), we work whether you're home or not. You'll get a text when we're done.
